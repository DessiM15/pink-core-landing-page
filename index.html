OUR STORY

A studio built on
strength & community.

PinkCore Pilates Studio was founded in 2019 with a simple belief: pilates should be accessible, empowering, and—yes—fun. What started as a small reformer studio in Montrose has grown into Houston's favorite boutique pilates destination.

We believe every body deserves to feel strong. Whether you're a first-timer or a seasoned practitioner, our certified instructors will meet you exactly where you are.

“Physical fitness is the first requisite of happiness.”

— Joseph Pilates

WHAT WE OFFER

Find your practice.

Six distinct class styles. One goal: help you feel incredible.

Mat Pilates

The foundation of it all. Build core strength, flexibility, and body awareness on the mat. Perfect for beginners and pros alike.

50 min All Levels

Reformer

Spring-loaded resistance meets precision movement. Our signature reformer classes sculpt, lengthen, and challenge you in the best way.

55 min Int / Adv

Barre Fusion

Pilates meets ballet meets burn. Small, isometric movements set to energizing music. You'll shake, you'll smile, you'll come back.

45 min All Levels

Prenatal Pilates

Gentle, safe, and empowering movement for expecting mamas. Modified exercises that support your changing body every trimester.

45 min All Levels

Power Pilates

High-intensity, low-impact. Combines classical pilates with cardio bursts and resistance training. Leave feeling powerful.

50 min Int / Adv

Private Sessions

One-on-one attention tailored to your goals. Injury rehab, sport-specific training, or just a personalized practice—it's all about you.

60 min Custom
